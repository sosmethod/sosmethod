There are millions of neurons in the brain, each producing its own electrical signals. These signals combined, generate brain waves. Human beings have 5 different types of these electric patterns. Each serves a purpose to help us cope with various situations – whether it is to process and learn new information or calm down after a stressful day.

Binaural beats cause the brain to match the frequency of the binaural tone. This is what is known as the “frequency following response”. Similar to the theory of tuning forks.

The frequencies used in the Relax and Deep Sleep meditations are either alpha, beta or theta to reduce hyperactivity and encourage our heart-rate, blood pressure and breathing to slow down. They promote pleasant states of mind that our body already recognizes. Be sure to listen on headphones to get the full effect.
